About Coffe

Indonesian coffee, which is coffee that is cultivated and exported from Indonesia, is currently ranked the fourth largest in the world in terms of production of 648,000 tons, after Brazil, Vietnam and Colombia. There are basically three kinds of coffee beans grown in Indonesia, namely Arabica coffee beans, Robusta coffee beans, and Liberica coffee beans. Coffee in Indonesia has a long history and has an important role for the economic growth of people in Indonesia. Indonesia is blessed with a geographical location that is very suitable to function as a coffee plantation. Indonesia's location is ideal for a microclimate for coffee growth and production.
